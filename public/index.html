Toys documentation

  • Get 10 toys press for example /toys
  • Get 10 toys by page: /toys?page=1
  • Search by name or info: /toys?s=child
  • Search by category: /toys/cat/catname
  • Search by price range: /toys/prices?min=150&max=200
